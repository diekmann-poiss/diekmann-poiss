About Diekmann & Poiss

We combine technology and nature to create sustainable, smart, and beautiful green spaces. Our mission is to empower architects, urban planners, and green building enthusiasts with innovative tools that simplify complex design challenges.

Whether you're designing a vertical garden for a residential building, planning a green roof installation, or integrating greenery into urban infrastructure, our platform provides the tools and insights you need to succeed.

Our Vision

To transform how the built environment integrates with nature by making sustainable green design accessible, intuitive, and rewarding for every professional.
